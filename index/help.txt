## Tickers:
Returns top tickers across discord or checks whether your ticker is valid.
```/tickers <action> <ticker?>```
- action: Desired action to use.
    - list: Returns top tickers today. ```/tickers action:list```
    - check: Returns whether ticker is valid and provides closest match. ```/tickers action:check ticker:^spx```
- ticker: Only required when checking.
## Prediction:
Takes a ticker and returns a 3mo prediction probability.
```/predict <ticker> <model?>```
- ticker: Valid Yahoo Finance ticker. (use /tickers to validate)
- model: Choose from Implied Volatility, Extrapolation, Aggregrate-Extrapolation, and Logical Analysis. ([more details](https://github.com/razoring/QuantumDiscordBot))
## Quote:
Tracks ticker infomation live; use refresh to quickly update
```/quote <ticker>```
- ticker: Valid Yahoo Finance ticker. (use /tickers to validate)
## Chart:
Returns the live quote and candlestick chart of the tickers up to the duration.
```/chart <ticker> <duration>```
- ticker: Valid Yahoo Finance ticker. (use /tickers to validate)
- duration: Choose from the list.
## Alerts:
Set reminders for when your ticker reaches a certain price or return the daily volatility.
```/alerts <action> <ticker/identifier> <price?>```
- ticker: Valid Yahoo Finance ticker. (use /tickers to validate)
- action: Desired action to use.
    - create: Creates an alert ```/alerts. action:create ticker:meta price:700```
    - list: List all active alerts with their identifiers. ```/alerts action:list```
    - delete: Deletes a specific alert with the identifier. ```/alerts action:delete identifier:123456790```
    - clear: Deletes all alerts. ```/alerts action:clear```
